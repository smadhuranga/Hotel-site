Welcome to Granbell Hotel Colombo


Where contemporary spaces meet island living

Granbell Hotel, Colombo is set minutes away from the Central Business District and overlooking the scenic Port City. Elegant interiors matched with unhindered views of the azure coast and its surroundings, from your bedroom or the rooftop pool and bar, our city hotel in Colombo, Sri Lanka promises you a relaxed island escape.

OUR LOCATION



We are located in the centre of Colombo’s Central Business District. Running parallel to the rustic railway line and steps away from Colpetty’s rail station on Marine Drive, enjoy your stay at one of the most unique luxury hotels in Colombo which provides you with easy, convenient access to the popular restaurants, shopping malls, and other attractions in Sri Lanka.
