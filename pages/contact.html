Frequently asked questions (FAQ)



    • Our guests are kindly requested to check in starting from 2 PM and are expected to vacate their rooms before 12 noon. Please note that early check-in and late check-out are subject to room availability, and additional charges may be applicable.

    • We are pleased to offer complimentary luggage storage services at Granbell Hotel. However, we kindly remind our guests that we cannot assume responsibility for any valuable items such as jewelry or electronic devices.

    • All our rooms consist of a comfortable chair, a conveniently sized table, and a well-positioned lamp. This setup provides ample space for guests to comfortably accommodate their laptops and organize their files.

    • Certainly, we have an elevator in our hotel that is equipped with wheelchair accessibility features to ensure convenience and ease of movement for all guests.

    • Absolutely, we have dedicated rooms that are specifically designed to be wheelchair accessible. To ensure availability, please reach out to our team via our hotline or email at reservations1@granbellhotel.lk or reservations2@granbellhotel.lk, and kindly inform us of your requirements.

    • We are pleased to offer a spacious parking area capable of accommodating vehicles of any size.

    • Regrettably, pets are not permitted at our hotel. We apologize for any inconvenience caused.

    • Unfortunately, we do not offer the option of an additional bed for double occupancy rooms. However, we do have a separate room category available for triple occupancy, which may be suitable for your needs. An additional fee will apply to the third occupant.

    • All of our rooms are designated as non-smoking rooms. To cater to our guests who smoke, we have designated smoking areas on the hotel premises. These areas include the terrace area of the lobby, the walkway at the restaurants, and the rooftop.

    • Certainly, our reception operates 24/7, allowing you to check in at any time, even during late-night hours.

    • Yes, we offer airport shuttle service for the convenience of our guests. For detailed information regarding the shuttle service and associated charges, please contact our reservations team at reservations1@granbellhotel.lk or reservations2@granbellhotel.lk.
